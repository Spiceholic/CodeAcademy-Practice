Karaage

Karaage is a luscious, crispy and juicy type of fried chicken typical for Japanese streetfood. It is made using mostly chicken thigh cut into bite-sized pieces, marinated in various sauces and spices and then deepfried in their typical batter.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken thigh
  • 50ml soy sauce
  • 3 gloves crushed garlic
  • 1 knob of grated ginger
  • 30ml mirin
  • 15g chicken bouillion powder
  • 15g mayonaisse
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 15ml water

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into bite sized pieces
  2. Mix all other ingredients, making the marinade
  3. Put chicken pieces into a bag and pour in the marinade, massage it in
  4. Let the chicken rest for 1h
  5. Mix equal parts AP flour and starch
  6. Dredge the marinated chicken pieces in to flour-starch mix and deepfry them
  7. Fry each piece for about 5 minutes
  8. Enjoy some tasty fried chicken!
